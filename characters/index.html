The Lunaris are a race of nocturnal felines dwelling on the continent of Ailiya. Their main deity is the Silver Sage, Éthir, the founder and protector of their nation, who discovered/ created the Moon Crystal (or Thiro Quarris) that gave them their magic and blessed their land. When Éthir disappeared, the Lunaris believed that their spirit was passed on to a new host, who is to become the new Silver Sage.

The Dominion of Rhyll is a nation of canines dwelling on the continent of Ailiya. Despite being neighbours with the Lunaris, the two nations remained isolated until the Rhyll were faced with an existential threat. They worship the war god known as Yion and hold a yearly tournament in his name to determine their ranks. Most Rhyll are not capable of using magic.
